# Code Of Conduct

Rare is committed to a respectful, professional, and technically rigorous community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in language and tone.
- Critique ideas, code, and designs without attacking people.
- Share context clearly when reporting bugs, security concerns, or protocol disagreements.
- Assume good intent, but be direct about risks, regressions, and unclear reasoning.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, doxxing, or threats.
- Spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Publishing private credentials, sensitive user data, or operational details without permission.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `conduct@rareid.cc`. If the issue is security-sensitive, use the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or reject content that violates this code of conduct, and may limit participation when repeated or severe violations occur.
